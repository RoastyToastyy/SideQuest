# SideQuest

A Gen Z volunteer platform that turns real-world impact into game-like side quests for non-profits. Submission for MLH Hackathon Social Good Week under "Helping a non profit" challenge.

## What is Side Quest?

Side Quest is a gamified volunteering platform where helping your community feels like completing quests in a game. The website is built using a comic book style, to make it feel more interactive and visually appealing to the young audience and Gen Z.

Instead of “volunteer work,” users pick side quests like:

- Feeding someone in need
- Leaving heartfelt notes to strangers
- Talking to a friend
- Supporting a community event

Each action becomes a mission with meaning.  
They may be tiny, but small actions carry huge impacts.

## Why this exists

Volunteering often feels:

- hard to discover
- disconnected from daily life
- not engaging for younger users

Side Quest reframes it:

what if doing good felt like playing a game you actually want to log into?

## Features

- Browse available volunteer “side quests”
- Complete real-world missions which are featured as "challenges"
- Write about your experience on the blog and get a chance to be featured
- Users optionally can send picture of proof
- Earn XP / completion feedback (gamified feel)
- Clean Gen Z-friendly UI
- Mobile-responsive design
- Instant interactions (no friction onboarding)

## Problem & Impact

Side Quest bridges the gap between:

people who want to help  
and organizations that need help  

By turning volunteering into small, achievable “quests,” we make social good:

more approachable  
more frequent  
more engaging
